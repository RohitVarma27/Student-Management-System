FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I access the practice quizzes?

A: Practice quizzes are available for registered users. Simply log in to your account and navigate to the "Practice Quizzes" section to access quizzes based on your selected subjects and topics.

Can I create custom study plans?

A: Yes, our platform allows you to create personalized study plans tailored to your specific needs and goals. You can select the subjects, topics, and study duration according to your preferences.

How do I track my performance on the platform?

A: Your performance metrics, including quiz scores, study progress, and time spent on each topic, are tracked automatically. You can view detailed performance reports in your dashboard to monitor your progress.

Are the questions in the question bank updated regularly?

A: Yes, we regularly update our question bank with new questions and revisions to ensure relevance and accuracy. Our team of experts continuously reviews and enhances the question database.
